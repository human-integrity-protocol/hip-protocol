🔥 Tier 1

Government ID Verification

Tier 1 is the highest assurance credential level. Because identity verification requires a paid third-party service, Tier 1 credentials are issued by independent pathway operators — not by hipprotocol.org directly.

Why a separate site? HIP is a protocol with no treasury and no fees (Charter DP-7). Tier 1 verification has a real cost, so it's provided by independent operators under DP-5 — Permissionless Proliferation. Anyone can build HIP-compatible verification services.
Verification Options
● Self-Service Verification
Verify your government-issued ID and complete a liveness check through hipverify.org. Takes 2–3 minutes. A small fee (~$0.50) covers identity verification processing.
Go to hipverify.org →
● Institutional Verification
If your organization (university, employer, government agency) operates a HIP-compatible verification service, they can issue your Tier 1 credential directly — no separate fee required. Ask your institution if they support HIP credentialing.
● Third-Party IDV Providers
Identity verification providers that integrate with hipverify.org can issue HIP-compatible credentials through their own platforms. As providers adopt the standard, more options will become available here.
How It Works
Regardless of which pathway you use, the result is the same: a Tier 1 HUMAN-PROOF credential saved in your browser, compatible with the attestation tools on hipprotocol.org.
1. Your identity is verified against a government-issued document.
2. A one-way deduplication hash is computed to enforce one-credential-per-human.
3. Your cryptographic credential is generated in your browser — the pathway operator never has your private key.
4. Your credential is saved to this browser's localStorage and auto-loads on hipprotocol.org.
Don't need Tier 1? Tier 2 (device biometric) and Tier 3 (peer vouch) are free and instant — available directly on hipprotocol.org. You can always upgrade later.
